Professional arc

From computer science to marketing technology leadership.

Luigi studied Computer Science and Engineering at Politecnico di Milano (BSc + MSc) before moving into analytics roles across travel, telecom, retail, and global platforms like TikTok. He blends product thinking with measurement rigor, ensuring every launch has clear data foundations.

He is fluent in GA4, Google Tag Manager, Adobe Analytics, Tealium, Adobe Target, and the data plumbing required to keep those stacks reliable. He regularly partners with engineering and legal teams to deliver privacy-aligned tracking.

How he works

  • Calm under pressure. Known for fixing tracking crises when the launch date is immovable.
  • Global collaborator. Comfortable leading distributed teams across Zurich, Milan, and Dubai.
