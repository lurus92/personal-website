ExamBot helps educators create personalized assessments and instantly review answers. It uses retrieval to keep question banks aligned to curriculum, and server-side scoring to avoid leaking prompts. Analytics instrumentation tracks question difficulty, completion rates, and feedback to improve the assistant over time.
